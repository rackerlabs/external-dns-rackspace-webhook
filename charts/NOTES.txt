Thank you for installing {{ .Chart.Name }}!

1. Provide Rackspace credentials:
   helm upgrade --install external-dns ./external-dns \
     --create-namespace -n {{ .Values.namespace }} \
     --set-string secret.username="YOUR_USERNAME" \
     --set-string secret.apiKey="YOUR_API_KEY"

2. Verify:
   helm status external-dns -n {{ .Values.namespace }}
   kubectl get pods -n {{ .Values.namespace }}
